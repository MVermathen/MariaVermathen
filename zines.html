Un toit c'est un droit, Un toi c'est la loi!

Zine of the occupation of the Kiosque des Réformés summer 2025 (July & August) for the right to housing of 43 deminorised unaccompanied minors in Marseille. All the texts, except for the back-cover text, were written or spoken by unaccompanied minors from the occupation or members of Binkadi. The illustrations were also created by various occupiers and supporters during the protest camp.
